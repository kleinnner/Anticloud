# Inte11ect Acceptable Use Policy

## 1. Prohibited Uses

The following uses of Inte11ect are strictly prohibited:

1. **Illegal Activities** — Any use that violates applicable local, national, or international law
2. **Harmful Content Generation** — Creating content that promotes violence, harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or self-harm
3. **Fraud and Deception** — Generating misleading content, impersonation, phishing, social engineering, or financial fraud
4. **Weapons Development** — Designing weapons, explosives, chemical/biological agents, or other harmful devices
5. **Surveillance** — Mass surveillance, facial recognition without consent, or unauthorized monitoring
6. **Critical Infrastructure** — Direct control of critical infrastructure systems (power grids, water systems, transportation, medical devices)
7. **Medical Advice** — Providing medical diagnoses or treatment recommendations without qualified human review
8. **Automated Decisions** — Making automated decisions that significantly impact individuals' legal rights or access to services without human oversight

## 2. Responsible Use Guidelines

- **Human Oversight**: Always review AI-generated outputs before acting on them, especially in high-stakes contexts
- **Transparency**: Disclose when content is AI-generated if sharing publicly
- **Data Privacy**: Do not submit sensitive personal information unless you have appropriate safeguards in place
- **Accuracy Verification**: Verify factual claims against authoritative sources before relying on them
- **Security**: Keep your instance updated and review .aioss ledger logs for unusual activity
- **Resource Consideration**: Be mindful of computational resources; avoid unnecessary high-frequency queries

## 3. Reporting Violations

If you encounter or suspect a violation of this policy:

- Open an issue at the project repository
- Contact the maintainers directly via the project's communication channels
- Include relevant .aioss ledger entry hashes and timestamps when reporting

## 4. Enforcement

Violations of this policy may result in:
- Temporary or permanent suspension of access
- Removal of content or data
- Notification to relevant authorities for illegal activities
- Public disclosure of violations (with appropriate privacy protections)

## 5. Disclaimer

Inte11ect is provided "as is" without warranty. The developers and contributors are not responsible for misuse of the platform. Users are solely responsible for ensuring their use complies with all applicable laws and regulations.
