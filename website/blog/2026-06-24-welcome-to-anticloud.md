# Welcome to the Anticloud Ecosystem

Today we're launching the Anticloud documentation portal — a unified home for 50+ open-source projects united by a shared cryptographic foundation.

{/* truncate */}

## What is Anticloud?

Anticloud is a sovereign technology research ecosystem. Every project in the ecosystem shares a common cryptographic foundation built on SHA3-256 hash chains, Ed25519 digital signatures, and the `.aioss` tamper-evident ledger format.

## 11 Platform Projects

From the [Kathon cryptographic browser](/docs/projects/kathon) with vision-LLM ad blocking to the [Kasteran systems language](/docs/projects/kasteran) with rune-based symbolic syntax, each project is designed for privacy, verifiability, and AI-native workflows.

## 40 Developer Tools

The ecosystem includes 40 developer tools organized into four domains: Security & Cryptography, Compliance & Governance, Analysis & Planning, and Developer Utilities.
