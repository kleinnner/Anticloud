# Glossary 26: Business Glossary

## Terms

### SaaS (Software as a Service)
- Cloud-hosted software with subscription pricing
- API-OSS offers both self-hosted and SaaS options

### Self-Hosted
- Customer runs API-OSS on their own infrastructure
- Full control, compliance, and data sovereignty

### Subscription
- Recurring payment for continued access
- API-OSS tiers: Free, Pro, Business, Enterprise, Government

### Tier
- Pricing level with defined features and limits
- Higher tiers add: users, models, compliance, support

### MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
- Predictable monthly subscription revenue
- Key metric for subscription businesses

### ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)
- MRR × 12 (normalized annual revenue)
- Standard SaaS valuation metric

### ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)
- Total revenue divided by customer count
- Target: $50+ for API-OSS

### Churn Rate
- Percentage of customers who cancel each month
- Target: <5% monthly

### LTV (Lifetime Value)
- Total revenue from a customer over their lifetime
- LTV = ARPU / churn rate

### CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- Cost to acquire one new customer
- Target: <$500

### LTV:CAC Ratio
- Relationship between customer value and acquisition cost
- Target: >5:1

### NRR (Net Revenue Retention)
- Revenue from existing customers including expansion minus churn
- Target: >120%

### Freemium
- Free tier with paid upgrades
- API-OSS: free for individuals, paid for teams

### Open Core
- Core product is open source, paid features are proprietary
- API-OSS monetization model

### Professional Services
- Billable services: training, fine-tuning, annotation
- High-margin revenue stream

### Partner / Reseller
- Third-party selling API-OSS to their customers
- Channel for geographic and vertical expansion
