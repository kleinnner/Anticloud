## ONE-PARAGRAPH DESCRIPTION

API-SOS (Agent-Predictive Intelligence Sovereign Operating System) is the world's first sovereign AI decision engine — a complete AI operating system that runs entirely on local hardware with zero cloud dependency, zero external API calls, and a cryptographically hash-chained audit trail. Built for regulated institutions that cannot legally use cloud AI (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google), API-SOS ensures no foreign government can turn off, audit, or restrict the AI decisions of its users.

## THE PROBLEM

Every major AI product (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Harvey) requires US cloud servers under US jurisdiction. Regulated institutions — sovereign wealth funds, central banks, government agencies, hospitals, law firms — cannot use these products for sensitive work. They have been forced to choose between AI capability and data sovereignty. Until now.

---

## THE SOLUTION

API-SOS is the only AI system built specifically for institutions that cannot use the cloud. It runs on any hardware — a laptop, a server, a bunker — with zero internet required. Every decision is recorded in the .aioss ledger, a SHA-256 hash chain that makes tampering mathematically detectable.

---

## KEY FEATURES

- **Zero cloud dependency** — Runs entirely on local hardware. No internet required.
- **.aioss ledger** — Hash-chained audit trail. Tamper-proof by design.
- **Knowledge graph** — Full provenance tracking for every AI decision.
- **Contradiction detection** — 3-layer engine catches hallucinations and inconsistencies.
- **Multi-agent council** — Risk, Legal, and Strategist agents deliberate every output.
- **Local LLM inference** — 5 GGUF models, 1.5B to 7B parameters.
- **Document ingestion** — PDF, Excel, Word, PowerPoint, CSV, JSON, HTML.
- **Sandboxed tools** — 11 tools with path traversal protection.
- **Open source** — Apache 2.0 license, publicly auditable.

## FOUNDER BIO — FULL

Lois Kleinner is the founder of API-SOS, the world's first sovereign AI operating system. At 22, he built a complete AI decision engine — from Rust backend to React frontend to LLM integration — that runs entirely on local hardware with zero cloud dependency.

Before founding API-SOS, Lois identified a critical gap in the AI market: every major AI product depends on US cloud servers under US jurisdiction, making them unusable for regulated institutions. He spent months building the alternative — a system that provides the full power of AI without the sovereignty compromise.

The product is fully built, open-source, and ready for deployment across government, finance, healthcare, and legal sectors.

Lois is based in Dubai and committed to building API-SOS as the UAE's flagship sovereign AI infrastructure company.
