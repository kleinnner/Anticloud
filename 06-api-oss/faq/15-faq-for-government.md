# FAQ for Government

## Classification

```text
Q: Can API-OSS handle classified data?
A: When deployed in appropriate environments with proper controls.

Q: What security clearances does your team have?
A: Team members with various clearance levels available.

Q: Do you support IL5/IL6?
A: Architecture supports IL5+. IL6 requires additional controls.
```

## Compliance

```text
Q: Is API-OSS FedRAMP authorized?
A: We provide the controls. FedRAMP authorization is per-deployment.

Q: Do you support NIST 800-53?
A: Yes, controls mapped to NIST 800-53 rev5.

Q: What about DoD SRG?
A: Architecture aligns with DoD Cloud Computing SRG.
```

## Deployment

```text
Q: Can we run on classified networks?
A: Yes, air-gapped deployment fully supported.

Q: Do you support TPM attestation?
A: Yes, hardware-based attestation for deployment integrity.

Q: What about supply chain security?
A: Signed releases, SBOM provided, reproducible builds.
```
