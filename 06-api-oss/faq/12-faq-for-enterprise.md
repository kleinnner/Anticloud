# FAQ for Enterprise

## Security

```text
Q: Is API-OSS SOC 2 compliant?
A: API-OSS provides the controls. You run on your infrastructure.
We provide SOC 2 Type II report for our SaaS.

Q: Do you support SSO?
A: Yes, SAML, OIDC, and LDAP. Okta, Azure AD supported.

Q: Can I use my own encryption keys?
A: Yes, BYOK is supported via HSM integration.
```

## Compliance

```text
Q: Is API-OSS HIPAA compliant?
A: You can deploy HIPAA-compliant with API-OSS on your infra.

Q: Do you have a DPA?
A: Yes, available at docs/legal/dpa.md.

Q: Where is data stored?
A: On your infrastructure. You control data residency.
```

## Support

```text
Q: What support plans do you offer?
A: Community (free), Team (8x5), Enterprise (24x7).

Q: Do you offer SLA?
A: Yes, 99.9% for Business, 99.99% for Enterprise.

Q: Is there phone support?
A: Enterprise tier includes phone support.
```
