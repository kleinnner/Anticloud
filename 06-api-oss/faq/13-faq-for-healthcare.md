# FAQ for Healthcare

## Compliance

```text
Q: Can API-OSS be used in HIPAA-compliant environments?
A: Yes. Deploy on your infrastructure with BAAs in place.

Q: What audit capabilities do you have?
A: Hash-chain audit logging, tamper-evident, exportable.

Q: Do you support PHI data handling?
A: All data stays on your infrastructure. No PHI leaves.
```

## Deployment

```text
Q: Can we run in an air-gapped environment?
A: Yes, full air-gapped deployment supported.

Q: Do you support on-premise deployment?
A: Yes, bare-metal, VM, Kubernetes, or Docker Compose.

Q: What about disaster recovery?
A: Multi-region federation with automatic failover.
```

## Integration

```text
Q: Can we integrate with Epic/Cerner?
A: Via custom plugins or data source connectors.

Q: Do you support HL7/FHIR?
A: Via custom transformers in the plugin system.
```
