# EU DIGITAL EUROPE PROGRAMME — GRANT APPLICATION

**Program:** Digital Europe Programme (DIGITAL) — AI Data & Infrastructure
**Call:** European AI Factories / AI-on-Demand Platform
**Project:** API-SOS — Sovereign AI for European Public Sector
**Applicant:** API-SOS FZCO (with EU entity/partner)
**Request:** €2-5M (Grant)

---

## 1. SUMMARY

The Digital Europe Programme funds strategic digital infrastructure for the EU. API-SOS proposes to establish a "Sovereign AI Factory" — a deployable, local-first AI decision engine for European public sector institutions.

**Current TRL:** 7
**Target TRL:** 9
**Duration:** 24 months

---

## 3. WORK PLAN

### WP1: EU Sovereign AI Factory (€1.5M — Months 1-8)
- Reference deployment architecture on EU data centers
- European language support (DE, FR, ES, IT, NL, PT)
- Integration with EU eIDAS digital identity
- EU-specific compliance module (GDPR, NIS2, Cyber Resilience Act)

### WP2: Public Sector Pilots (€1.5M — Months 6-18)
- 3 EU public sector pilots: municipal government, healthcare, education
- Document integration with EU public administration systems
- Performance benchmarking on EU cloud infrastructure

### WP3: Open Source EU Community (€1M — Months 6-24)
- EU developer community building
- EU university partnerships (3 research collaborations)
- Sovereign AI certification program for EU engineers

### WP4: Sustainability & Scale (€1M — Months 12-24)
- EU entity formation
- Partnership with EU cloud providers
- Revenue model for EU public sector
