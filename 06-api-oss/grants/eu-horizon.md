# EU HORIZON EUROPE — GRANT APPLICATION

**Program:** Horizon Europe — Cluster 4: Digital, Industry & Space
**Call:** AI for Sovereign Digital Infrastructure
**Project:** API-SOS — European Sovereign AI Decision Engine
**Applicant:** API-SOS FZCO (with EU consortium partners)
**Request:** €5-10M (Grant)

---

## 1. SUMMARY

API-SOS proposes to deploy the world's first sovereign AI operating system across European regulated institutions. The project will localize API-SOS for EU requirements — GDPR compliance, EU language models, European data center deployment, and integration with EU digital identity frameworks.

**Duration:** 36 months
**Consortium:** API-SOS (coordinator) + 3-5 EU partners (TBD)
**TRL start:** 7 → **TRL end:** 9

---

## 2. PROBLEM

European institutions face a strategic dependency on non-EU AI infrastructure:

- All major AI products depend on US cloud servers (US jurisdiction)
- GDPR Articles 44-49 restrict personal data transfers to third countries
- EU Digital Decade policy targets digital sovereignty by 2030
- No European AI product provides a complete sovereign AI operating system

**The gap is not AI capability — it's AI sovereignty.** European banks, hospitals, and government agencies must choose between AI adoption and legal compliance.

---

## 3. SOLUTION

API-SOS provides the complete solution:

| Requirement | API-SOS Capability |
|-------------|-------------------|
| GDPR-compliant architecture | Zero data leaves customer hardware |
| AI audit trail | .aioss hash-chained ledger |
| Local inference | Runs entirely on EU-located hardware |
| No third-party dependency | No OpenAI, Google, AWS required |
| Enterprise features | Knowledge graph, contradiction detection, council system |

## 4. WORK PACKAGES

### WP1: EU Localization (€1.5M — Months 1-12)
- EU language model fine-tuning (German, French, Spanish, Italian, Dutch)
- GDPR compliance certification
- Integration with EU eIDAS digital identity framework
- EU data center deployment patterns

### WP2: Enterprise Hardening (€2M — Months 1-12)
- SOC 2 Type II + ISO 27001 certification
- EU-specific encryption standards (E2E, AES-256)
- Penetration testing (3 independent EU firms)
- EU Cybersecurity Act compliance

### WP3: Pilot Deployments (€2.5M — Months 6-24)
- 5 EU pilot deployments across sectors:
  - 1 EU government agency
  - 1 EU financial institution
  - 1 EU healthcare provider
  - 1 EU legal firm
  - 1 EU research institution
- Reference architectures for each deployment type

### WP4: EU Ecosystem (€2M — Months 6-36)
- EU system integrator partnerships (Atos, Capgemini, Sopra Steria)
- Open-source community building in EU
- Academic partnerships (4 EU universities)
- Training and certification for EU AI engineers

### WP5: Scale-Up (€2M — Months 12-36)
- EU entity formation
- Sales team across key EU markets
- Customer success infrastructure
- 12 additional EU language models
