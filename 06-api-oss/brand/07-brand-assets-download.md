## Overview

Download API-OSS brand assets.

```yaml

  - apioss-logo-horizontal.svg
  - apioss-logo-horizontal.png
  - apioss-icon.svg
  - apioss-icon.png (512x512)

  - apioss-slide-deck.pptx
```
