# Kasteran* — Code of Conduct
© Lois-Kleinner & 0-1.gg 2026

## Overview

The Kasteran* community is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and considerate
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be collaborative and open to different perspectives
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards other community members

### Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Offensive comments related to personal characteristics
- Sexual content or unwelcome advances
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sustained disruption of discussions or events
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all Kasteran* community spaces, including:
- GitHub repositories (issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Community forums and mailing lists
- Chat channels (Discord, IRC, Slack)
- Social media accounts
- In-person events (meetups, conferences, hackathons)
- Official correspondence

## Reporting Process

### How to Report
If you experience or witness a violation:
1. **Contact the moderation team**: conduct@kasteran.dev
2. **Provide details**: Include what happened, when, and any relevant context
3. **Optional**: Request confidentiality

### What to Include
```
report:
  date: 2026-06-19
  location: "GitHub issue #1234"
  description: "Comment [link] contained ..."
  impact: "Made me feel unsafe because ..."
  witnesses: ["user1", "user2"]
  evidence: [screenshots, links]
```

### Confidentiality
All reports are handled confidentially:
- Reports are only shared with the moderation team
- Your identity will be protected
- Retaliation for reporting is prohibited

## Enforcement

### Review Process
1. Report is received and acknowledged within 24 hours
2. Moderation team investigates the incident
3. All parties are given opportunity to respond
4. Decision is made based on community standards
5. Outcome is communicated to the reporter

### Consequences
Violations may result in:

| Severity | First Offense | Repeat Offense |
|---|---|---|
| Minor | Private warning | Public warning |
| Moderate | Public warning | Temporary ban |
| Severe | Temporary ban | Permanent ban |

### Appeals
Decisions can be appealed:
```
appeal@kasteran.dev
```

## Moderation Team

The moderation team consists of:
- Community managers
- Core team members
- Volunteer moderators

All moderators are trained on:
- Code of Conduct enforcement
- Conflict resolution
- Cultural sensitivity
- Unconscious bias

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1, and the [TODO Group](https://todogroup.org/) Open Code of Conduct.

## Conclusion

The Kasteran* community is dedicated to providing a positive and safe environment for all members. This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations and the process for addressing violations. Together, we can build a welcoming community where everyone can contribute and thrive.
