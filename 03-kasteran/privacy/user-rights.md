# Kasteran* — User Rights
© Lois-Kleinner & 0-1.gg 2026

## Overview

Kasteran* respects and supports all user data rights under GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy regulations. This document explains how to exercise your rights regarding personal data collected by Kasteran*.

## Your Rights

### Right to Access
You have the right to know what personal data we hold about you.

**How to exercise:**
```
Submit access request to: privacy@kasteran.dev
Include: Your name and email address
Response time: Within 30 days (GDPR), 45 days (CCPA)
```

**What you will receive:**
- Categories of data collected
- Specific data items
- Purposes of processing
- Third parties with access
- Retention periods

### Right to Correction
You have the right to correct inaccurate personal data.

**How to exercise:**
```
Submit correction request to: privacy@kasteran.dev
Include: What data to correct and the correct information
Response time: Within 30 days
```

### Right to Deletion
You have the right to request deletion of your personal data.

**How to exercise:**
```
Submit deletion request to: privacy@kasteran.dev
Include: Your name and email address
Response time: Within 30 days (subject to legal exceptions)
```

**Exceptions:**
- Legal retention requirements
- Active legal claims
- Public health reasons
- Freedom of expression

### Right to Portability
You have the right to receive your data in a portable format.

**How to exercise:**
```
Submit portability request to: privacy@kasteran.dev
Include: Preferred format (JSON, CSV, etc.)
Response time: Within 30 days
```

### Right to Object
You have the right to object to processing based on legitimate interests.

**How to exercise:**
```
Submit objection to: privacy@kasteran.dev
Include: What processing you object to and why
Response time: Within 30 days
```

### Right to Restrict Processing
You have the right to request restriction of processing.

**How to exercise:**
```
Submit restriction request to: privacy@kasteran.dev
Include: What processing to restrict
Response time: Within 30 days
```

### Right to Withdraw Consent
If processing is based on consent, you can withdraw at any time.

**How to exercise:**
- Compiler telemetry: `kasteran config set telemetry.enabled false`
- Cookie consent: Cookie settings on our website
- Marketing: Unsubscribe link in emails

## How We Process Requests

### Verification
We take reasonable steps to verify your identity:
- Email verification
- Multi-factor authentication (for account holders)
- Additional verification for sensitive requests

### Timeline
```
Day 1: Request received
Day 2: Identity verified
Day 5: Request assessed
Day 15: Request executed
Day 20: Confirmation sent
```

### Fees
- First request per year: Free
- Additional requests: Reasonable fee if manifestly unfounded
- Excessive requests: May be charged or refused

## Automated Decisions

Kasteran* does not make automated decisions that significantly affect users based solely on automated processing. If automated decision-making is implemented, users will be informed and have the right to human review.

## Complaints

If you are unsatisfied with our response:

### Internal Review
File a complaint with our DPO:
```
dpo@kasteran.dev
```

### Regulatory Authority
If you are in the EU, you can file a complaint with your local data protection authority.

## Data Protection Officer

Our DPO can be contacted:
```
Email: dpo@kasteran.dev
Address: Kasteran* Privacy, 0-1.gg, [Address]
```

The DPO is responsible for:
- Monitoring compliance
- Advising on data protection
- Cooperating with supervisory authorities
- Handling data subject requests

## Conclusion

Kasteran* supports all user rights under applicable privacy regulations. Users can exercise their rights by contacting our privacy team or DPO. We process all requests promptly and transparently.
