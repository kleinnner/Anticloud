# Kasteran* — Privacy Policy
© Lois-Kleinner & 0-1.gg 2026

## Overview

This privacy policy explains how Kasteran* collects, uses, shares, and retains personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices. This policy applies to the Kasteran* compiler, tooling, website, and related services.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account information**: When you create an account on the Kasteran* website or package registry
- **Communication data**: When you contact support, participate in forums, or report issues
- **Contributions**: When you contribute code, documentation, or translations

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage data**: Anonymous information about compiler usage and commands (opt-in)
- **Crash reports**: Anonymized crash data (opt-in)
- **Website analytics**: Page views, referrer, browser type (with consent)

### Information from Third Parties
- **Authentication providers**: If you use SSO (Google, GitHub, etc.)
- **Package registries**: Information about published packages

## How We Use Information

### Purpose of Processing
We use your information to:
- **Provide the service**: Operate the compiler, package registry, and website
- **Improve the product**: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, optimize performance
- **Communicate**: Respond to support requests, send updates, notify of changes
- **Comply with law**: Fulfill legal obligations, respond to lawful requests

### Legal Basis
We process data based on:
- **Consent**: For optional telemetry and marketing communications
- **Contract**: To provide the services you request
- **Legitimate interest**: To improve and secure our services
- **Legal obligation**: To comply with applicable laws

## How We Share Information

### Service Providers
We share data with trusted service providers:
- Cloud infrastructure providers (hosting)
- Code repository hosting (GitHub)
- Package distribution networks
- Analytics providers (anonymized data only)

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if:
- Required by law, court order, or legal process
- Necessary to protect rights, property, or safety
- To enforce our terms of service

### Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred.

## Data Retention

We retain your information for:
- **Account data**: As long as your account is active
- **Usage data**: 90 days (aggregated indefinitely)
- **Crash reports**: 1 year
- **Communication records**: 3 years
- **Legal compliance**: As required by applicable law

## Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have rights to:
- **Access**: Know what data we hold about you
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a portable format
- **Object**: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Restrict**: Request restriction of processing
- **Withdraw consent**: Withdraw consent at any time

## Cookies

We use minimal cookies:
- **Essential**: Session management, security (always active)
- **Analytics**: Anonymous usage tracking (opt-in)
- **Preference**: Store your preferences (opt-in)

## Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Encryption at rest (AES-256)
- Access controls and audit logging
- Regular security assessments

## International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses for EU data subjects.

## Children's Privacy

Kasteran* is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes to this policy:
- Via email (if you have an account)
- Through the Kasteran* website
- Via the compiler (on update)

## Contact

For privacy-related inquiries:
- **Email**: privacy@kasteran.dev
- **DPO**: dpo@kasteran.dev
- **Address**: Kasteran* Privacy, 0-1.gg, [Address]

## Regulatory Compliance

This policy complies with:
- **GDPR**: General Data Protection Regulation (EU)
- **CCPA**: California Consumer Privacy Act (US)
- **LGPD**: Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (Brazil)
- **PIPEDA**: Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (Canada)
- **APPI**: Act on Protection of Personal Information (Japan)
