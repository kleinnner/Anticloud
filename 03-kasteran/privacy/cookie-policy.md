# Kasteran* — Cookie Policy
© Lois-Kleinner & 0-1.gg 2026

## Overview

This cookie policy explains what cookies Kasteran* uses, their purpose, duration, and how to opt out. We use minimal cookies and respect your privacy choices.

## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help websites remember your preferences, understand usage patterns, and improve your experience.

## Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies
These cookies are necessary for the website to function:

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration | Opt-Out |
|---|---|---|---|
| session_id | Session management | Session | Required |
| csrf_token | Security (CSRF protection) | Session | Required |
| auth_token | Authentication state | 24 hours | Required |

### Analytics Cookies
These cookies help us understand how the website is used:

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration | Opt-Out |
|---|---|---|---|
| _ga | Google Analytics user ID | 2 years | Yes |
| _gid | Google Analytics session | 24 hours | Yes |
| _gat | Google Analytics rate limiting | 1 minute | Yes |

### Preference Cookies
These cookies remember your preferences:

| Cookie | Purpose | Duration | Opt-Out |
|---|---|---|---|
| theme_pref | Dark/light mode preference | 1 year | Yes |
| lang_pref | Language preference | 1 year | Yes |
| telemetry_consent | Telemetry preference | 1 year | Yes |

## Cookie Consent

### Consent Mechanism
When you first visit the Kasteran* website, you will see a cookie consent banner:

```
This site uses cookies to improve your experience.
[Accept All] [Reject All] [Customize]
```

### Customization
You can customize your cookie preferences:
```
Customize Cookies:
[x] Essential (required)
[ ] Analytics
[ ] Preferences
[Save Preferences]
```

### Changing Preferences
You can change your cookie preferences at any time:
- Click the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer
- Adjust your browser settings
- Use our preference center

## Third-Party Cookies

We use minimal third-party cookies:

### GitHub
When you use GitHub authentication:
- GitHub may set cookies for authentication
- These are governed by GitHub's privacy policy

### Package CDN
When you download packages from our CDN:
- The CDN may set performance cookies
- These are anonymous and used for CDN optimization

## Cookie Duration

| Duration | Description |
|---|---|
| Session | Deleted when you close your browser |
| Persistent | Remain for a set period |
| 24 hours | Deleted after 24 hours |
| 1 year | Deleted after 1 year |
| 2 years | Deleted after 2 years |

## How to Opt Out

### Browser Settings
You can control cookies through your browser:
- **Chrome**: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies
- **Firefox**: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
- **Safari**: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
- **Edge**: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

### Do Not Track
We support the Do Not Track (DNT) browser signal. When DNT is enabled, we only use essential cookies.

### Opt-Out Links
- **Google Analytics**: [Google Analytics Opt-Out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- **Network Advertising**: [NAI Opt-Out](https://optout.networkadvertising.org)
