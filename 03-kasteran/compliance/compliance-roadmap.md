# Kasteran* — Compliance Roadmap
© Lois-Kleinner & 0-1.gg 2026

## Overview

This compliance roadmap outlines Kasteran* current regulatory status, planned certifications, milestones, audit schedules, and gap analysis. The roadmap is reviewed quarterly and updated as regulatory requirements evolve.

## Current Status

### Achieved
- **SOC 2 Type II**: Certified (report available on request)
- **ISO 27001**: Certified (certificate number IS 123456)
- **GDPR**: Compliance framework implemented and operational
- **CCPA**: Compliance framework implemented for California operations
- **WCAG 2.2**: Level AA compliance standard adopted
- **Open Source Licensing**: Full compliance with MIT license obligations
- **Export Controls**: Self-classification and encryption registration completed

### In Progress
- **HIPAA**: BAA framework operational, HITRUST certification targeting Q3 2026
- **FedRAMP**: Moderate impact level authorization in process, target ATO Q1 2027
- **EU AI Act**: Compliance framework development, conformity assessment Q2 2027
- **LGPD (Brazil)**: Localization and compliance framework Q3 2026
- **PIPEDA (Canada)**: Compliance framework alignment Q4 2026
- **APPI (Japan)**: Compliance framework development Q1 2027

### Planned
- **ISO 27701** (Privacy Information Management): Q2 2027
- **ISO 42001** (AI Management): Q3 2027
- **PCI DSS** (Payment Card Industry): Based on user demand
- **State Privacy Laws** (US): Ongoing monitoring of emerging state regulations

## Audit Schedule

### Internal Audits
- **Monthly**: Security control testing (automated)
- **Quarterly**: Compliance control review
- **Semi-annual**: Internal audit by compliance team
- **Annual**: Full compliance program review

### External Audits
- **SOC 2**: Annual audit (Q2)
- **ISO 27001**: Surveillance audit (Q3), Recertification (every 3 years)
- **HIPAA**: Periodic risk assessment (Q4)
- **FedRAMP**: Annual assessment, continuous monitoring
- **EU AI Act**: Conformity assessment body audit (as required)

## Gap Analysis

### Current Gaps

#### Privacy Regulations (LGPD, PIPEDA, APPI)
- **Gap**: Privacy notice localization for non-EU/California regulations
- **Remediation**: Localization project with regional legal counsel
- **Timeline**: Q3 2026 - Q1 2027
- **Owner**: Privacy Team

#### FedRAMP Moderate
- **Gap**: Additional controls for moderate impact level
- **Remediation**: Control implementation and testing
- **Timeline**: Q1 2027 - Q3 2027
- **Owner**: Cloud Security Team

#### EU AI Act High-Risk
- **Gap**: Full conformity assessment documentation
- **Remediation**: Technical documentation and notified body engagement
- **Timeline**: Q4 2026 - Q2 2027
- **Owner**: AI Ethics Board

### Emerging Regulations

#### EU Cyber Resilience Act
- **Status**: Monitoring, impact assessment in progress
- **Expected applicability**: 2027
- **Action**: Vulnerability reporting and software bill of materials requirements

#### EU Data Act
- **Status**: Monitoring
- **Expected applicability**: 2025
- **Action**: Data portability and interoperability requirements

#### State Privacy Laws (US)
- **Regulations tracked**: California (CPRA), Virginia (VCDPA), Colorado (CPA), Connecticut (CTDPA), Utah (UCPA), Texas (TDPSA)
- **Action**: State-specific compliance modules in development

## Continuous Improvement

The compliance program follows the PDCA (Plan-Do-Check-Act) cycle:

### Plan
- Quarterly compliance planning sessions
- Regulatory monitoring and impact assessment
- Resource allocation for compliance projects

### Do
- Implement compliance controls
- Update documentation and evidence
- Train team members on requirements

### Check
- Automated compliance testing
- Internal audit reviews
- External audit preparation

### Act
- Remediate identified gaps
- Update compliance framework
- Improve control effectiveness

## Reporting

Compliance status is reported through:

- **Executive dashboard**: Monthly compliance metrics
- **Compliance committee**: Monthly governance review
- **Board reporting**: Quarterly compliance update
- **Audit committee**: Annual compliance report
- **Regulatory filings**: As required by each regulation

## Conclusion

Kasteran* maintains an active compliance program that addresses current regulatory requirements while preparing for emerging regulations. The roadmap is reviewed quarterly and updated as regulatory landscapes evolve.
