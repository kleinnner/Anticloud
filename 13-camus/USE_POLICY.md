# Camus Acceptable Use Policy

## Prohibited Uses

1. **Illegal activities** — Any violation of law.
2. **Harmful content** — Violence, harassment, hate speech, discrimination.
3. **Fraud** — Impersonation, phishing, spam, deceptive practices.
4. **Weapons development** — Chemical, biological, or nuclear weapons.
5. **Surveillance** — Mass surveillance without consent.
6. **Medical advice** — Clinical decision support without professional validation.

## Responsible Use

- Do not rely on Camus for high-stakes decisions without verification.
- Review outputs for accuracy on factual claims.
- Respect intellectual property rights.
